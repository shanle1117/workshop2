- I need information
- Tell me something
- What can you do?
- What are your capabilities?

**Multi-Intent Questions:**
- I want to register for BCSAI, what are the fees?
- Tell me about AI programs and career opportunities
- What are the admission requirements and fees for BCSCS?
- How do I contact someone about registration?

**Follow-up Questions (Context Dependent):**
- Tell me more
- What else?
- Can you elaborate?
- Give me more details
- What about [related topic]?

**Typo/Informal Questions:**
- wat programs r available?
- how 2 apply?
- tell me bout fees
- who cn i contact?

**Very Specific Questions:**
- What is the exact CGPA requirement for BCSAI for local students with STPM?
- What is the email address of the dean?
- What is the phone number for the faculty office?
- What is the exact address of FAIX?

**Comparison Questions:**
- What is the difference between BCSAI and BCSCS?
- Which program is better for me?
- Should I choose AI or cybersecurity?

**Hypothetical Questions:**
- If I have CGPA 3.5, can I apply?
- What if I don't have MUET?
- Can I apply if I'm an international student?

**Negative Questions:**
- I don't want to register
- I'm not interested in AI
- I can't find the information

**Very Long Questions:**
- I'm a student who is interested in studying artificial intelligence and I want to know about the programs available at FAIX, the admission requirements, the fees, and the career opportunities after graduation, can you help me?

**Questions with Numbers:**
- What is the fee for 4 years?
- How many staff members are there?
- What is the duration in years?

## üí° Usage Tips

1. **Start with Basic Questions**: Test greetings and simple queries first
2. **Test Each Intent Category**: Go through each category systematically
3. **Test Multi-Language**: Verify language detection and responses
4. **Test Context Continuity**: Ask follow-up questions to test context
5. **Test Edge Cases**: Try ambiguous, complex, and unusual queries
6. **Verify Accuracy**: Check that responses are factually correct
7. **Check Response Format**: Ensure URLs, formatting, and structure are correct
8. **Test Performance**: Note response times for different query types
